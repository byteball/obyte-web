Legend

Lo Scontrino Vale

10% In Moneta Digitale

Take the fiscal receipt here, and bring to a "Distribution Point" by the next business day (Mon-Sat). To be valid for the purposes of our distribution, the receipt must be such and which one is delivered to you by the merchant, keep it well without damaging it in any way. The merchant does not offer discounts, simply adheres to claiming his fiscal receipt.

Punto Distribuzione Bytes

In this store you can go to claim your free bytes, brings your byteball address and your fiscal receipt of the stores of the "Lo Scontrino Vale" initiative. The measurement parameter of the distribution is based on how much you have spent, you will receive your cashback immediately: 10% in Bytes. After you receive cashback the receipt will be invalidated.

Punto Distribuzione Bytes + Qui La Spesa Vale

In this shop you can do the same of "Punto Distribuzione". The "Qui La Spesa Vale" means how much you spend here you will receive cashback immediately : 10% of how much you spent, 20% if you paid with Bytes.
